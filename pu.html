Exponent Practice Questions with Answers

Q1: Simplify (x³y⁻²)(x⁵y⁴)

Answer: x⁸y²

Explanation: Combine like bases: x³⁺⁵ = x⁸ and y⁻²⁺⁴ = y²

Q2: Simplify (a²b³)²

Answer: a⁴b⁶

Explanation: Apply power: a²² = a⁴ and b³² = b⁶

Q3: Simplify (m³n⁴)/(m⁵n²)

Answer: m⁻²n²

Explanation: Subtract exponents: m³⁻⁵ = m⁻² and n⁴⁻² = n²

Q4: Simplify (2x²y³)(3xy⁴)

Answer: 6x³y⁷

Explanation: Multiply constants: 2×3=6, x²×x=x³, y³×y⁴=y⁷

Q5: Simplify [(p²q³)/(pq)]³

Answer: p³q⁶

Explanation: Inside: p²/p = p, q³/q = q² → (pq²)³ = p³q⁶
