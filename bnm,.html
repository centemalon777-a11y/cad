Practice Questions

Question 1: What is 20% less than 40?
Answer: 32
Explanation: 20% less than 40 means subtract 20% of 40 from 40. 20% of 40 is 8, so 40 - 8 = 32.
Question 2: If a room charge is 150N and there is a 6.5% state tax, what is the total cost?  Answer: 159.75N
Explanation: Tax is 6.5% of 150N = 9.75N. Total = 150 + 9.75 = 159.75N.
